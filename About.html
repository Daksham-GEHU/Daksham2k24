What is Daksham2k23

"DAKSHAM2K23" is a state level Intercollegiate management fest which is being organised by School of Management of Graphic Era Hill University Dehradun to exhibit their managerial talent by participating in various competitions crafted to showcase their skills, competent talent and to be the part in various competitive events, boost confidence, experience learning and exponential growth and win attractive hampers and prizes.


The motive of Daksham2k23 is to encourage student's participation from various universities and colleges which gives them an opportunity to promote healthy competitions among the budding managers which headturn to be future leaders and glorify in their careers.


This event aims to enclose from creativity to innovation, planning to strategy, fun-learning through various management activities and competitions. It is a blend of wide spectrum of formal and informal events:

  • Daksham2k23 is a 3 days management fest which will provide a platform to students across Uttarakhand to exhibit their managerial talents.
  • Daksham2k23 aims to have more than 50,000+ students from various colleges across Uttarakhand.
  • The flagship event of Daksham2k23 that sets a stage of igniting the minds. illuminating the future leaders and inspiring the visionary entrepreneurs. The event hosts pioneering leaders who have proven their leadership mettle in respective domains through unconventional and disruptive ways.
