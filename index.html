About Event
  • The fest will take place on an offline mode for UG & PG students.
  • Participants can register as a group for the activities and can take part in as many events they want.
  • Students participating have to carry their college ID Cards for verification process.
  • The participants will have to reach GEHU, Dehradun Campus for the fest.
  • The students will be responsible for their travel commutation, accommodation & food.
Students are welcomed with open hearts and we hope you learn, enjoy, participate with full enthusiasm.
